## What SolarOps Does

This isn't a basic contact list. Here's what the platform covers today:

**Sales & Lead Management**
The sales pipeline is gamified — reps earn XP and levels as they move leads through stages. There's a Lead Lobby for incoming prospects, full interaction tracking (calls, emails, SMS, notes, meetings), and a leaderboard to keep the team motivated. This alone replaces tools most companies pay $50–$150/user/month for.

**Customer Management — 360° View**
Every customer has a complete profile: contact history, work order history, profitability, SolarEdge site ID, PowerCare case tracking, and full activity logs. You can see everything that's ever happened with a client in one place.

**Work Order Lifecycle**
Work orders go through a full pipeline — from draft to quote sent, quote approved, scheduled, in progress, completed, invoiced, and paid. Each work order tracks labor, parts, contractor costs, revenue, and profit margin. Quotes are generated from within the platform.

**Contractor Portal**
Contractors have their own login. They see their assigned jobs, submit completion reports, upload site photos (organized by category — before, after, serial numbers, voltage tests, etc.), and submit invoices. Admin reviews and approves from the main dashboard.

**SolarEdge Monitoring & Alerts**
The platform connects to SolarEdge's API and pulls in site alerts automatically. Each alert has a severity level and can be acknowledged, resolved, or linked directly to a work order. This means no more manually checking the SolarEdge portal.

**Billing & Invoicing**
Invoices are created within the platform and sync with Xero. Payment tracking, overdue flags, and revenue reports are all built in.

**Inventory Management**
Equipment, tools, and provider management — all tracked in one place.

**Ops Center Dashboard**
A real-time dashboard that shows today's schedule, unbilled jobs, team activity, profitability metrics, work order status counts, and notifications — all at a glance.

**Multi-User Roles**
The platform supports Admin, COO, Technician, Sales, and Support roles, each with appropriate access levels. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
