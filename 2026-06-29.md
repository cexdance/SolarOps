## Deploy: Recolor "assigned" calendar cards (follow-up to dimming fix)

#### `fa1f054` — fix(so-calendar): recolor "assigned" cards from slate gray to indigo
- **Problem**: After the dimming fix shipped, the calendar still looked "grayed out." Root cause was NOT dimming (already fixed, blue `new` cards rendered vivid on the same July days, proving cards were not dimmed). It was the STATUS COLOR: `assigned` work orders used `bg-slate-100 text-slate-700 border-slate-200` (washed-out slate). Since most of the board is `assigned` (dispatched to a contractor), the whole calendar read as disabled/gray.
- **Fix**: Recolored `assigned` in `WorkOrderCalendar.tsx` — card `bg-indigo-100 text-indigo-700 border-indigo-200`, status dot `bg-indigo-500`. Indigo is distinct from new (blue), in_progress (amber), completed (green), invoiced (purple), paid (emerald), archived (gray).
- **Files**: `src/components/WorkOrderCalendar.tsx`
- **Verified**: preview 7-Day view, Jun 29 – Jul 5, all cards readable, footer shows v1.7.5.4 • db 06-29.
- **PATTERN**: when users say a board is "grayed out," check the status color map before assuming a dimming/opacity bug. A pale slate status fill reads as disabled even at full opacity.
